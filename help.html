Getting Started

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your details. You'll need a valid email address and a secure password.

Go to your dashboard and use the "Log Eco Action" form. Select an action type, add a description, and click "Log Action" to earn your first points!

You can log recycling, green transportation, energy conservation, water saving, and nature activities like tree planting. Each action type has different point values.

Points & Rewards

Points are awarded based on the environmental impact of your actions. Recycling earns 10 points, biking to work earns 15 points, and planting trees earns 20 points.

Visit the Rewards page to browse available rewards. Click "Redeem" on any reward you can afford. You'll receive redemption details via email.

No, your points never expire! You can save them up for bigger rewards or use them as you earn them.

Badges & Achievements

Badges are earned automatically when you complete specific milestones, like logging your first action, maintaining streaks, or reaching point thresholds.

A daily streak is the number of consecutive days you've logged at least one eco action. Maintaining streaks can earn you special badges and bonus points.

Yes! Visit the Badges page to see all available badges, your progress toward earning them, and which ones you've already unlocked.

Account & Privacy

Go to your Profile page and click "Change Password" in the Account Actions section. You'll need to verify your current password first.

Yes, you can control your privacy settings in your Profile page. Toggle off "Show on Leaderboard" to keep your profile private.

Account deletion is available in your Profile page under Account Actions. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
